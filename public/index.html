How This Works

    

      Your password is never sent to the server.

      Instead, your browser computes a SHA-256 hash of your password — a one-way fingerprint — and only the hash is transmitted.


      The instructor's dashboard will then use brute force to try all possible birthday dates and find which one matches your hash.
      This shows why predictable passwords (like birthdays) are insecure even when "protected" by hashing.
